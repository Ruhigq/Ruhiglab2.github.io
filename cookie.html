Cookie Policy for Ruhig'Home

Last Updated: September 19, 2023

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. These cookies allow us to distinguish you from other users of our website, which helps us to provide you with a better experience and also allows us to improve our site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits, as well as compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future. Cookies are also used to enhance your experience, for example by remembering your login details, thereby making your interaction seamless.

Your Control of Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. Detailed information about cookie management can generally be found in the web browser's settings or help menu. However, if you disable cookies, some parts of our service may not function properly.

Third-party Cookies

In some cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. For instance, this site uses Google Analytics to gather anonymous data such as the number of visitors to the site, and the most popular pages. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit.
